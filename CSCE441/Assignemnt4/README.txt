SPIDER CONTROLS
---------------------------
MOVE: Forward/Backward
    up and down ARROW keys

TURN: Left/Right
    left and right ARROW keys


CAMERA CONTROLS
---------------------------
TURN: Up/Down/Left/Right
    w , s , a , d

ROTATE: Clockwise/Counter
    q , e

PAN: Up/Down/Left/Right
    W , S , A , D

MOVE: Forward/Back
    E , Q

ADJUST LIMBS
----------------------------

Click and Drag the mouse up and down

Pressing 1 ,2 ,3 will change at which joint the limbs are changed.


MOVE HEAD
------------------------------
Press 5

Click and Drag the mouse up/down left/right
